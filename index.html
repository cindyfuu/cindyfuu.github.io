Dish One: Sweet Sour Ribs

Source: Xia Chu Fang

Ingredients:

  • Pork Ribs: 500g
  • Salt: 3g
  • Cooking Wine: 15ml
  • Soy Sauce: 30ml
  • White Vinegar: 45ml
  • Sugar: 60g
  • Sliced Ginger

Optional Ingredients:

  • Green Onion

Steps:

  1. Clean and wash pork ribs.
  2. Add 100g vegetable oil and sliced ginger in a wok with high heat.
  3. Put pork ribs into the work and stir fry until pork ribs becomes brown color.
  4. Add 15ml cooking wine, 30ml soy sauce, 45ml white vinegar and 60g sugar and then stir fry.
  5. Add 200 c.c. water into the wok with medium heat about 20 minutes.
  6. After 20 mins, add 3g salt and to stir fry on high heat for few minutes and it is ready to serve.
